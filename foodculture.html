Thai Cuisine

In Thai culture, food plays a big part in a social occasion. In daily life, rice is the staple Thai food at every meal.

Chinese Cuisine

There is more emphasis on how to eat to balance yin and yang.

French Cuisine

French food relies on simple combinations of rich, natural flavors that come together to create unforgettable, internationally-renowned dishes.

Spanish Cuisine

Each Spanish dishes has its own origins,it all come together with using the most flavorful spices.

Greek Cuisine

The perfect go to for a healthy choice. The culture that offers flavorful dishes yet is good for your diet.

Indian Cuisine

Indian cuisine consists of a variety of regional and traditional cuisines native to the Indian subcontinent.

Italian Cuisine

Wine, cheese and pasta are important parts of Italian meals. Pasta comes in a wide range of shapes such as penne, spaghetti, linguine, fusilli and lasagna.

Japanese Cuisine

Japanese cuisine is based on combining the staple food, which is steamed white rice or gohan, with one or more okazu, "main" or "side" dishes.
